About Me

Raised in the Bahamian out islands, I grew up with the web as my window to the world. Armed with a computer and a connection to the internet, I felt transported beyond the confines of the 100-person islands on which I lived and into a realm of possibility, utility, purpose, and knowledge. As a developer, I want to create the experiences that first opened my eyes to the globe.

Previously, I have been in positions requiring creativity, hard work, and problem solving. Today, I utilize these skills and many more to create meaningful experiences between people and technology. With a wealth of experience in diverse fields ranging from code to customer satisfaction to carpentry, I am a creative problem solver who continuously strives to improve my skills and knowledge. I love to create and build, turning ideas into reality through code.

Gem Jam

A browser based game built using the Phaser.io framework and HTML5. Exploring a 2D platformer world, the player must locate gems while navigating dangerous pitfalls and traps. The game features a timer as well as a score to compare previous attempts. I used the application Tiled to help me in generation a large 2D side scrolling world using an open source, small-sized spritesheet to allow for quick loading times. The game uses states to keep the code organzied and easy to use. All the assets are preloaded for quickness once the actual game has begun.

Mind Dump

Input an email, statement, text message, or simply stream-of-consciousness writing. Even though this is writing online, it's competely private. There is no right or wrong way to do it. Don't edit, don't overthink, just let your brain dump.

Mind Dump breaks your text down into emotion, social, and language categories to help you gain more insight. It gives you an analysis of the overall tone of the content.It utilizes IBM's Watson platform, leveraging its tone analysis engine to provide data-driven insight into one's inner thoughts to better communicate, gain perception, live a more mindful day.

Accio Bike

A registry for your bicycle to proactively prevent and recover from bike thefts. Without creating an account, a user can search the database of missing bikes for information like a bicycle's unique serial number, brand, model, color, size, or the zip code from which it went missing. If your bike has gone missing, create a free account and fill in your bike's information. Searching Craigslist for a used bike to purchase? Check our missing registry to ensure you're not buying someone else's stolen property.
